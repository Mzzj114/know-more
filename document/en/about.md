# About Know More

In today's rapidly evolving landscape of generative AI, the ability to use large language models efficiently and reliably is becoming an essential skill. However, for most learners, "Prompt Engineering" still presents a high barrier to entry, with unclear practical pathways and fragmented best practices that are difficult to systematically acquire and reuse.

Know More was initiated as an open-source, non-profit project in response to this challenge. We are dedicated to organizing scattered and abstract prompt engineering techniques into structured, reusable learning resources and practical pathways. Through clear examples, actionable templates, and progressive learning designs, we help users understand and master the core methods of prompt engineering in real-world tasks.

At Know More, users can not only systematically learn relevant knowledge but also practice and validate their skills around specific scenarios, gradually developing stable and transferable competencies. Meanwhile, the community gathers practical experiences from various domains, allowing users to quickly find suitable prompt templates based on actual tasks and continuously optimize them through exchanges and feedback.

We believe that the value of technology lies in its accessibility to more people. Know More aims to lower the barriers to AI adoption through openness and collaboration, enabling every learner to leverage large language models more efficiently and controllably, expanding their cognitive boundaries and practical capabilities.
