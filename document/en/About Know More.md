# About Know More

With the rapid development of generative artificial intelligence today, the efficient and reliable use of large models is becoming an important foundational capability. However, for most learners, "prompt engineering" still has high barriers to entry, unclear practical paths, and high-quality experiences that are difficult to acquire and reuse systematically.

Know More is an open-source public service project initiated against this backdrop. We are dedicated to organizing scattered and abstract prompt techniques into structured, reusable learning resources and practical paths. Through clear case studies, actionable templates, and progressive learning designs, we help users understand and master the core methods of prompt engineering in real-world tasks.

At Know More, users can not only systematically learn relevant knowledge but also practice and verify it in specific scenarios, gradually building stable and transferable usage capabilities. At the same time, the community gathers practical experiences from different fields, allowing users to quickly find suitable prompt templates based on actual tasks and continuously optimize them through exchange and feedback.

We believe that the value of technology lies in being understood and used by more people. Know More hopes to lower the barrier to using artificial intelligence through open collaboration, enabling every learner to utilize large models more efficiently and controllably, expanding their cognitive boundaries and practical capabilities.
